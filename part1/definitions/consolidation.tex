\begin{definition}[Consolidation] \label{def:consolidation} \index{Consolidation} \index{Consolidated model}
  To compare, finding points in common and differences between, models of the same and different type. This way designers together create \emph{consolidated} models. See \emph{teamwork} or \emph{collaboration} in any english dictionary. \cite[p. 301]{benyon10}
\end{definition}
